The theme is designed around having a quad core PC with 2 graphics cards but is easily customizable.
If you want to use CoreTemp instead of SpeedFan, select each of the Text items under the "Cores" section and change their "class" to "LH_Monitoring/CoreTempText". Then change the status images to "LH_Monitoring/CoreTempStatusImage".

Similarly, to convert this theme from using MSI Afterburner to using RivaTuner or GPU-Z to monitor your graphics card(s) select the items under the GPUs heading and change them accordingly.
